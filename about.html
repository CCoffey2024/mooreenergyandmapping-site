Moore County Mapping and Energy Alliance · d/b/a Moore Energy & Mapping

Rooted in Moore County.
Built to serve the Sandhills.

We are a North Carolina nonprofit bringing professional-grade geospatial analysis, precision mapping products, and energy resilience resources to the communities of Moore County and the greater NC Sandhills region — without the price tag that usually goes with it.

Mission

Why we exist

Moore County and the surrounding Sandhills region are rich in land, community, and natural resources. They are not rich in access to the kind of professional geospatial services that shape land use decisions, trail infrastructure, energy planning, and environmental stewardship in better-resourced places.

Moore Energy & Mapping was organized to close that gap. We apply 30+ years of geospatial intelligence, remote sensing, and applied GIS expertise at the community scale — producing the maps, data, and analysis that local government, nonprofits, land trusts, and residents need but rarely have access to.

Everything we produce is built on open-source tools and open data. Every deliverable is released under open licenses. The work stays in the community.

We are organized exclusively for charitable, scientific, and educational purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. No private benefit. No hidden agenda. The data is yours.

What we stand for

Grounded Rooted in Moore County, not abstract. We work from the land outward — local problems, local context, local benefit.
Open Open data. Open source. Open license. What we build, we publish. No paywalls on public benefit work.
Rigorous Community audience, professional standards. Every product is spatially accurate, properly attributed, and built to last.
Accessible Expert-grade tools, community-grade language. We don't put jargon walls between people and the information they need.
Resilient Built for long-term community benefit, not funding cycles. The organization and its data products are designed to endure.

Legal and operational structure

The organization's Articles of Incorporation include the dissolution and limitation-of-activities clauses required for 501(c)(3) recognition. IRS Form 1023-EZ has been submitted for federal tax-exempt determination.

Technical Partner

BlueLens Analytics LLC

Moore Energy & Mapping's primary technical partner is BlueLens Analytics LLC — a cleared geospatial intelligence consultancy registered in North Carolina and operated by the organization's Board Chair, Christopher Coffey.

BlueLens provides the technical horsepower behind Moore Energy & Mapping's more advanced deliverables: SAR change detection pipelines, remote sensing analysis, automated geospatial workflows, and defense-grade spatial analytics. The two organizations operate in tandem — BlueLens as the for-profit technical arm, Moore Energy & Mapping as the nonprofit community-facing vehicle for grants, outreach, and mission-driven work.

This structure keeps the community programs organizationally distinct from commercial consulting, while ensuring access to the highest-caliber geospatial expertise available in the Sandhills region.

Data sources & acknowledgments

Built on open data

All Moore Energy & Mapping geospatial products are built on openly licensed public data. We do not use proprietary base data, and all derived products are released under open licenses consistent with the terms of their source datasets.

NC OneMap North Carolina statewide orthophoto imagery and administrative boundary data. NC CGIA · Public domain
USGS National Map Digital elevation models, hillshade, hydrography, and topographic data. QL1 1m DEM where available. USGS · Public domain
OpenStreetMap Trail geometry, paths, amenities, and land use features via Overpass API and QuickOSM. © OSM contributors · ODbL
CARTO Background basemap tiles for cartographic context and reference. © CARTO · CC BY 3.0
QGIS Primary GIS software for all data production, cartographic layout, and spatial analysis. QGIS.org · GPL v2
OSGeo Foundation Open source geospatial software ecosystem. Moore Energy & Mapping is an OSGeo community organization. osgeo.org
