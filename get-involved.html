What we are not looking for

  • Map parties, mapathons, mapping hackathons, or any event where people show up uninvited with laptops and enthusiasm
  • Volunteer coordinators, community engagement consultants, or anyone whose job title contains the word "outreach"
  • Unsolicited partnership proposals from organizations that found us on a nonprofit database
  • Requests to speak at conferences about our work (we are too busy doing it)
  • Anyone who wants to "pick our brain" over coffee without a specific question

We are a small shop doing focused, unglamorous work for Moore County. We like it that way. If your reason for reaching out is specific, practical, and related to what we actually do — you will hear back promptly. Otherwise, the open data is right there and requires no introduction.
